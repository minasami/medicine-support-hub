# Company registration, verification, and medicine contributions

Medicine Support Hub gives pharmaceutical and healthcare companies a governed way to claim or create an official company profile and submit attributable medicine knowledge.

## Company user journey

1. Open `/industry` and sign in or create an account controlled by the company representative.
2. In **Find your company**, type the company name. Search results show the matching dataset company, medicine-record count, and available location context.
3. Select the correct existing record. Choose **New or currently unlisted company** only when no reliable match exists.
4. Submit the company type, country, city, work email, representative role, official website, authority evidence URL, and any naming or verification notes.
5. Automated checks generate an advisory score and risk signals. A platform administrator remains responsible for the final approval or rejection.
6. After approval, the representative can maintain the official company profile and submit medicine additions, product updates, corrections, evidence, educational resources, patient-support programs, and partnership opportunities.
7. Every contribution requires a clear title, detailed summary, identified product where relevant, and at least one official source or evidence URL.
8. Contributions remain attributed to the verified company and become public only after moderator review.

## Search behavior

The existing-company field is an accessible searchable combobox. It supports name, source-name, and slug matching, ranks exact and prefix matches first, and limits the rendered result list for mobile performance. Selecting a dataset company can prefill company type, country, city, and website when those values are available.

## Verification evidence

Strong requests include:

- the representative's real name and role;
- a company-domain work email when available;
- an official company website or recognized registry;
- an official staff page, authorization document, or other evidence of authority;
- notes explaining naming differences or corporate relationships.

Automated verification assists moderation but does not establish legal ownership or regulatory approval.

## Medicine contribution readiness

The contribution form displays a four-part readiness check:

- clear contribution title;
- detailed requested change and rationale;
- product identification for additions and updates;
- official source or evidence URLs.

Product additions and updates cannot be submitted without the product name and verifiable evidence.

## Safety boundary

Company verification and publication establish attribution inside Medicine Support Hub only. They do not establish regulatory approval, product registration, clinical suitability, price validity, inventory availability, or batch quality.
